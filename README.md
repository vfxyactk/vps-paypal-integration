## The Hidden Costs Most People Miss When Buying a PayPal VPS

Before anything else — let's talk money traps, because the VPS hosting world has a few classics.

**The introductory pricing bait-and-switch.** A host advertises "$3/month," you sign up, love it, then renew at $9/month. Many hosts do this. It's legal. It's also annoying.

**Bandwidth overage charges.** Some providers let you exceed your monthly data limit and then slap you with an overage bill. With certain hosts, this can be genuinely shocking. Always check: what happens when you hit your cap?

**Auto-charging your PayPal.** Here's one most people don't think to ask: does the host auto-charge you, or do *you* control when payments happen?

**Hidden setup fees, IP fees, backup fees.** These add up. What looks like a $5/month plan becomes $8/month with all the optional-but-required extras.
